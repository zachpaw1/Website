ZAC HOLLAWAY

ABOUT ME

Hi there! I'm Zac, and I'm a recent graduate from the University of California: Santa Cruz! I have a BA in their Art and Design: Games and Playable Media (AGPM) program. I was born and raised in the greater LA area, I love to go out and play practically any sport, whether that's football, beach volleyball, or basketball. I'm a sucker for coffee, and love to go out and try any and all local coffee shops I can find. I also enjoy reading, and of course, gaming with my friends. Gaming as a whole has been a big part of my life: from creating assets, to making music, and even just playing them, games and the industry have been with me for as long as I can remember. I've had many impressionable moments playing games growing up, and they have led me wanting to get into the industry myself. I want to give the next generation of people the exact same experiences I've had. Now, freshly out of university, with my degree in hand, I aim to do just that.

Here, you can see some of the work that I've done over the past few years at UCSC. I've primarily done sprite work, character models and animations, and music. These projects are mostly through classes I've taken, and serve as final projects after 10 week classes. If you'd like to see the animations and the music, please feel free to check out my Artstation linked in the Portfolio section!
